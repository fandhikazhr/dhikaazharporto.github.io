SAFMC 2023 - D1 Semi Autonomus Drone

Our team participated in SAFMC 2023, one of the most prestigious drone competitions in Southeast Asia. The competition challenges participants to develop innovative drone systems and push the boundaries of autonomous flight technology.

My Team on SAFMC 2023

Our team of three worked tirelessly to design, build, and test our drone system. We integrated various technologies to enable the drone to operate with minimal manual input, ensuring precise and stable flight performance.

Category: D1 - Semi Autonomous Drone

In this category, we developed a system that allows a drone to be controlled without using a conventional remote controller. Our goal was to create a semi-autonomous control system that enhances operational efficiency and usability.

🏆 4th Place in D1 - Semi Autonomous Drone Category

Alhamdulillah, our hard work paid off as we secured 4th place in the competition. Competing against top teams, we demonstrated innovation and technical expertise, making this achievement a proud milestone for our team.
